What Is Known

Désir–Goyal–Segev–Ye (2020) prove the capacity-constrained problem is APX-hard (so no PTAS unless P=NP) and give constant-factor approximation algorithms for the cardinality- and capacity-constrained versions. The exact best-possible ratio — closing the gap between the achievable constant and the APX-hardness threshold — is open.
