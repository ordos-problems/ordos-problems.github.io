Statement & Model

A simple 12\tfrac1221​-selectable online contention resolution scheme is known for the rank-1 matroid (single-choice) against the standard online adversary. \textbf{Open:} For the rank-1 matroid, does there exist a 12\tfrac1221​-selectable OCRS against the almighty adversary (which knows all realized values in advance)? An ex-ante prophet inequality guarantees 12\tfrac1221​ against the almighty adversary on rank-1 matroids, but the best-known OCRS against the almighty adversary is only 14\tfrac1441​ (Feldman et al. 2021). Either show a 1/2-selectable OCRS exists, find a modified one, or prove impossibility; even beating 1/4 is open.

What Is Known

For rank-1 matroids, Feldman, Svensson, and Zenklusen give a 1/41/41/4-selectable OCRS against the almighty adversary. In contrast, the corresponding ex-ante prophet-inequality guarantee is 1/21/21/2, so the OCRS and prophet views do not currently match quantitatively in this adversarial model. It is open whether one can close the gap all the way to 1/21/21/2, or even improve the OCRS constant beyond 1/41/41/4.
