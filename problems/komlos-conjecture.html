Statement & Model

What Is Known

The conjecture predicts a universal O(1)O(1)O(1) bound, independent of both the dimension and the number of vectors. Banaszczyk's theorem gives the best classical general bound, O(log⁡n)O(\sqrt{\log n})O(logn​), and Bansal, Dadush, and Garg made this algorithmic. Bansal and Jiang recently improved the dependence to O((log⁡n)1/4)O((\log n)^{1/4})O((logn)1/4).
