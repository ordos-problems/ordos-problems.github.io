Short Statement

A gambler faces nnn independent rewards from known but different distributions arriving in uniformly random order, accepting or irrevocably rejecting each and keeping at most one. What is the exact best-possible competitive ratio against the expected maximum?

Open Problem

Determine the exact prophet-secretary constant for independent non-identical rewards arriving in uniformly random order.
