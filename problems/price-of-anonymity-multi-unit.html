Short Statement

Statement & Model

Consider revenue maximization with nnn buyers whose value distributions differ. The optimal (Myerson) mechanism may charge different buyers different prices, whereas an anonymous mechanism must offer everyone the same price. For kkk identical units and nnn independent, non-identical regular value distributions, the price of anonymity is ρk=sup⁡FOPT(F)AP(F),\rho_k=\sup_F \frac{\mathrm{OPT}(F)}{\mathrm{AP}(F)},ρk​=Fsup​AP(F)OPT(F)​, where OPT(F)\mathrm{OPT}(F)OPT(F) is the optimal (Myerson) revenue and AP(F)\mathrm{AP}(F)AP(F) is the best anonymous posted-price revenue, and the supremum is over all valid instances FFF. For a single item (k=1k=1k=1) the tight value is ρ1≈2.62\rho_1\approx 2.62ρ1​≈2.62. What Is Known

For one item, the price of anonymity is known exactly up to numerical evaluation: ρ1≈2.6202\rho_1\approx 2.6202ρ1​≈2.6202.
